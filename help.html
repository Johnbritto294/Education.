Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?
Browse our course catalog from the My Courses page and click on any course that interests you. Hit the "Enroll Now" button on the course detail page. If it's a paid course, you'll be guided through the checkout process. Free courses are available instantly after enrollment. You can also use the search bar on the Dashboard to discover courses by topic, instructor, or skill level.
Can I download course materials?
Yes! Most courses offer downloadable resources such as PDFs, slide decks, code templates, and project files. Look for the 📥 Resources tab within each lesson. Video downloads are available on our Premium and Pro plans for offline viewing through the Stackly mobile app. Note that some instructor materials may be view-only due to licensing.
How do I get my certificate?
Once you complete all modules, quizzes, and the final assessment of a course, your certificate is automatically generated. Navigate to Progress → Certificates to view and download it as a PDF. Certificates include a unique verification ID and QR code that employers can use to verify authenticity. You can also share your certificates directly to LinkedIn from your profile.
What payment methods are accepted?
Stackly accepts all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, RuPay), UPI payments, net banking, and popular wallets like Paytm and Google Pay. International students can also pay via PayPal. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. EMI options are available on select plans for orders above ₹3,000.
How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see the email, check your spam/junk folder. You can also reset your password from Settings → Security while logged in. For extra security, we recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA).
Can I switch my role?
Absolutely! If you're a student and want to become an instructor, head over to Settings → Account → Role Management and apply for an instructor account. You'll need to submit a brief profile and at least one sample lesson for review. Our team typically reviews applications within 2–3 business days. You'll retain full access to your student courses even after switching roles.
